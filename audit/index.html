How we fix your SEO

What we do? What’s in our SEO package?

Step 1

Run a technical site audit

We check your site for issues that hurt performance, like crawl errors, slow load times and broken links. Basically, anything that keeps search engines or users from moving smoothly.

Step 2

Content and structure check

We make sure your content isn’t confusing search engines. If there’s duplicate content or mixed signals in your structure, we’ll fix it so everything’s clear and crawlable.

Step 3

Check the backlink profile

We analyze your backlink profile to find shitty links as well as new opportunities to grow your online presence the right way.

Step 4

Find the source of the symptoms

We don’t just point out issues but also find the reasons behind them, so you know what’s really holding your site back.

Step 5

Send a clear, detailed report

We’ll provide a comprehensive audit with easy-to-follow recommendations to improve your SEO and achieve better results.
